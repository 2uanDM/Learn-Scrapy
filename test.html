STEEL REBAR
Product Manual →
Rebar is the common name for hot-rolled ribbed steel bar, as revealed by its designation “HRB.” Rebar is extensively used in civil engineering projects such as buildings, bridges, and roads.
Futures
Options

Steel Rebar Futures Contract

Rebar futures was listed on March 27, 2009, one of the first steel futures products in China. Since China is the world’s biggest producer and consumer of steel, its rebar futures market is also among the largest globally. SHFE rebar futures serves the need of all players in the steel value chain for risk management and price discovery.
